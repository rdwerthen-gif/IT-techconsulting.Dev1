About Us

IT-Tech Consulting provides professional diagnostics, repairs, maintenance, and installations for all your technology needs. Our services are delivered by trained personnel and certified professionals with over 16 years of experience in the tech sector. We can source whatever you need; our operations leverage the use of existing hardware suppliers for IT and electronic products. We mainly focus on servicing IT infrastructure and Industry 4.0 technologies.

We offer a complete project solution, from minor repairs to large-scale installations, servicing systems in all sectors. We know the local landscape, and we are ready to assess and effect solutions wherever you are located within the Cape Town Metropolitan and surrounding areas, please refer to our service area map below.

Services

We offer customized IT Infrastructure and Industry 4.0 solutions as a service.

By Implementing tailored hardware, software, networks, and electronic systems specifically for your unique needs, for private home setups and small businesses to large-scale enterprise networks, we ensure that your systems operate as intended.

We also specialize in telematics and smart systems that are applicable to private homes and vehicles, business properties, vehicle fleets, appliances and machinery. This is achieved by integrating Internet of Things (IoT), Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), and Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT) technologies to optimize your operations and bring your vision to life.

Furthermore, we provide technical support for all commercial and proprietary electronic systems, for pre-existing or new installations, offering hardware inspections, repairs, maintenance, alterations, and modifications.

Workstation Support: We specialize in solving software and hardware issues for laptops and desktops, and installing upgrades to boost speed and extend the life of the machine.

Software Deployment & Support: We manage the efficient rollout and ongoing support for all enterprise software applications, including essential SaaS platforms like Microsoft 365, AWS, etc.

SOHO (Small Office/Home Office) Setups: We help small businesses work smarter from home; we set up reliable internet, secure networks, printers, computer workstations, and scalable tech so that you can focus on work instead of IT problems.

Hardware Lifecycle Management: Our services cover the entire lifecycle of physical IT assets, from sourcing and installation to repairs and maintenance of computers, servers, and peripherals.

End-User Technical Support: We offer prompt, professional on-site and remote help desk services, resolving technical issues to minimize employee downtime.

Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery: We safeguard critical data through rigorous backup protocols and comprehensive recovery plans designed to maintain operations in the face of disruption.

Server Management & Support: We install, configure, monitor, and maintain your core office servers, ensuring critical business systems remain operational and efficient.

Network Infrastructure Management: We engineer reliable and secure network environments (LAN, WAN, Wi-Fi), managing all necessary hardware installations and continuous performance monitoring.

Cloud infrastructure & Migration: We specialize in transitioning digital assets to scalable cloud platforms (public, private, or hybrid), optimizing operations and enhancing accessibility.

Smart Parking: Servicing basic parking lot access and ticketing systems, to advance systems guiding drivers to available parking spots using sensors, displays, and real-time data, reducing search times and traffic.

Waste Collection: We provide services for smart bins and refuse collection systems fitted with sensors that monitor waste management services, optimizing collection routes and schedules.

Energy Management: Servicing smart grids, UPS systems, and meters that control, monitor, measure, and optimize energy consumption in private homes and large scale buildings, and often integrating renewable sources like solar power systems.

Smart Lighting: Let us upgrade your lights to networked lighting systems with motion and/or ambient light sensors to control light circuits on activity and remotely, significantly saving energy.

Smart Greenhouses: Servicing greenhouses and vertical farms, IoT sensors and automated control systems that create and maintain optimal growing conditions.

Networked Appliances and Systems: We specialize in servicing appliances and systems that rely on wireless technologies, from terrestrial communication systems (UHF and VHF), satellite networking (data networks, DStv, OVHD, WANs, etc.), to IT and the Internet of Things (IoT) networks for residential and commercial environments.

Machine connectivity and monitoring systems: We service electronic monitoring systems, Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), and Operational Technology systems utilizing wired and/or wireless networks (including 5G, Wi-Fi, LoRaWAN, cellular, Zigbee, UHF, VHF, WirelessHART, NB-Iot, etc.).
